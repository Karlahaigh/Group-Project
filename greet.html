Fresh Vegetables Galore

As long as I can remember I have enjoyed foods from countries surrounding the Mediterranean Sea; foods from Greece, Italy, Spain...oh who am I kidding? I just like food!

But, one of the things that truly draws me to the foods influenced by the countries of the Mediterranean are the ingredients. Lots of fresh fruit and vegetables, olives, nuts, the hearty flavors, and glorious aromas...and wine! These coutries are also known for the strong family ties; the presiding matriarch who oversees her family, yet clearly demonstrates her love for them by bringing them to meals at the family table. These are images which come to my mind when I see and smell these foods. That is the inspiration behind this restaurant. Bringing families together. Our food trucks are simply a manner of bringing yummy food to you!
